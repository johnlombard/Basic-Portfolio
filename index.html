About Me

Hi! My name is John, and if I'm not coding or studying finance, you can find me surfing or hiking. I always enjoy a goodread, and am always open for suggestions. I attended University of North Carolina-Wilmington. North Carolina will alwaus have a place in my heart. I was born in the Philadelphia area, and look to establish a career here.

I recently started coding, but have been interested ever since I was introduced to a programmer on Wall Street who said his job was to put his co-workers out of a job. Sometime the truth hurts, but having technology skills has become a necessity in achieving your goals.

I'm a naturally curious person, and love to learn many a different things. In the future, I hope to learn the craft of shaping surfboards. I also enjoy challenging myself physically as well as mentally. In the near future, I intend on embarking on my greatest challenge of biking across the country. Wrightsville Beach, NC to Haystack Rock, OR. Only 3,300 miles! Please join me!
